What is the efficiency?
The efficiency is a dimensionless number that measures the effictiveness of a mechanism or machine in transforming the power input to the device to power output.
How to calculate efficiency?
To calculate efficiency you should use the following formula:
Efficiency = useful power out / total power
Why use efficiency?
The efficiency is used in order to minimize the wasted energy as much as possible
